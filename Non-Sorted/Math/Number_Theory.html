Number Theory ​

Number theory is the study of the properties and relationships of numbers. It is a branch of mathematics that deals with integers and their properties, such as divisibility, prime numbers, and congruences. Number theory has applications in cryptography, computer science, and other areas of mathematics

Number theory began with the manipulation of numbers, that is, natural numbers (N), and later expanded to integers (Z) and rational numbers (Q)
