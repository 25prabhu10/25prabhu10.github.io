The Accounting Equation:

Assets=Liabilities+Equity

Where:

  • Assets: Resources owned by the company (e.g., cash, inventory, property)
  • Liabilities: Obligations owed to others (e.g., loans, accounts payable)
  • Equity (a.k.a. Shareholders' Equity): Owner's claim on the assets after liabilities are deducted (e.g., common stock, retained earnings)
